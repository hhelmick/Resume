I am a PhD student in the department of Food Science at Purdue University. My reserach has the broad goal of developing relationships between structural bioinformatic models of pea protein and physical characterization used in food science. This has led to understanding of gels and emulsions critical to meat and dairy analogue production. Techniques include FTIR, DSC, Zeta potential, SDS-PAGE, and rheology. Physical modifications are conudcted using extrusion at sub zero temperatures. Coding and data analysis includes Python, RStudio, and Chimera. Prior to my time at Purdue University, I worked in lean six sigma based process improvement where I obtained by black belt in lean six sigma.
